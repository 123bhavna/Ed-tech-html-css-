Abandoned Baby pattern

The Abandoned Baby Pattern is a potent reversal signal, featuring a gap and a Doji between consecutive candlesticks.


This pattern suggests a significant shift in market sentiment, often marking the end of a trend and the beginning of a new one.


Tips to recognize -

1. Identify Gaps: Look for a gap between the previous and following candles.
2. Spot the Doji: Observe a Doji candle in the middle, signaling market indecision.
3. Isolation from Previous Trend: The pattern should be isolated from the prior trend, emphasizing a potential trend reversal.

Uptrend Abandoned baby

An uptrend Abandoned Baby signals a potential trend reversal, marking the end of the upward movement.


Trading implications -

1. Reversal Signal: Strong indication of an upcoming trend reversal from bullish to bearish.
2. Entry Point: Consider entering a short position as the downtrend begins after the abandoned baby pattern. Set a stop-loss above the pattern's high to manage risk effectively.
3. Exit Point: Exit the trade as the bearish trend strengthens, optimizing profit potential.

Downtrend Abandoned baby

An Abandoned Baby pattern during a downtrend signals a potential bullish reversal in the market.


Trading implications -

1. Bearish Reversal Signal: Downtrend Abandoned Baby serves as a strong bearish reversal indicator.
2. End of Downtrend: Marks a potential conclusion to the existing downtrend.
3. Entry/Exit Point: Consider buying after confirmation of the reversal. Set an exit point or stop-loss just below the Iow of the abandoned baby pattern.

Interesting Facts

1. Rare Occurrence: The Abandoned Baby pattern is relatively rare, adding to its significance when identified on a price chart.
2. Isolation from Prior Trend:Effectiveness is enhanced when the pattern is isolated from the preceding trend, emphasizing its standalone significance.
3. Applicability Across Timeframes: Can be observed on various timeframes providing flexibility for traders with different trading preferences.
